Krispy Kreme has a better taste. Krispy Kreme has more of a sweet taste, unlike Dunkin Donuts, where it taste more like bread than a donut. Krispy Kreme also tastes better when you reheat it for only like 5 seconds or so. When you reheat a Krispy Kreme Donut, it tastes like you just bought it fresh from the oven. But Dunkin Donuts tastes like it's soggy when you try to heat it up.
